PETER MASEN TABLE TENNIS ACADEMY

Peter Masen Table Tennis Academy offers exceptional table tennis programs, coaching and practice for any age and skill level.

Peter Masen Table Tennis Academy is highly recognised and respected in the table tennis society and provides a great environment for all players of different levels. Peter Masen is a well respected coach in the table tennis community. He has been coaching the state and national team for many years and has countless years worth of coaching and playing experience.

PMTTA students have been participating in state and national events for a very long time. We guarantee that we will provide you with the best quality training for students who want to compete or play for leisure.
