Peter Masen Table Tennis Academy is highly recognised and respected in the table tennis society and provides a great environment for all players of different levels. Our students have been participating in state and national events for a very long time. We guarantee that we will provide you with the best quality training for students who want to compete or play for leisure.

Peter Masen is a well respected coach in the table tennis community. He has been coaching the state and national team for many years and has countless years worth of coaching and playing experience.
